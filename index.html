About the Studio

BUILT ON
INTENT

Studio Varga was founded on a single premise: every structure should have a reason to exist beyond shelter. We approach each project as a conversation between material, light, and the people who will inhabit the space.

Based in Madrid, we work across residential, commercial, and cultural typologies. Our process is obsessively iterative — we refine until the building feels inevitable.
